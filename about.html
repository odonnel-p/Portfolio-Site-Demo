About Patrick

Patrick J. O'Donnel is a designer, artist and instructor.

As an artist, he believes that play and creativity in sculpture and technology are at the heart of innovating in the world of urban planning and architecture. As a designer, he believes that great conversations and a shared faith in the lab/studio environment is paramount to a professional creative process. As a instructor, he believes that good design is at the crux of engaging in discussions of creative practice and relevant work.

At various points in his career, he has called himself a...

  • graphic designer
  • user-interaction designer
  • user-experience designer
  • data visualization specialist
  • multimedia designer
  • researcher
  • new media artist
  • cartoonist
  • choreographer
  • sculptor

In his spare time, Patrick enjoys board games, architecture tours, trying to one-up his grandmother's baked good recipes, drum and bugle corps and the rule of threes.
